Tribute

May 2025

1. Abstract

A Tribute is a reflection of a user's Acts of Consumption, recorded on the network. It is created by combining one or more of the user's Consumption Units (CUs) into a single unit, which is then submitted through the Wallet App.

The Tribute is considered valid if submitted within 24 hours after local day end.

On Green Days, all tributes are eligible for Lysis, with unrecognised tributes also eligible for Touch. On Red Days all tributes are eligible for Touch only.

During the Lysis, accounts that have offered Tributes may be recognized with Nods. Each Nod includes its own Floor Price condition: Gratis (a non-transferable fungible token) will be issued against that Nod when the market price of the Coen rises above its assigned Floor Price.

3. System Logic

Note: See Consumption Unit

  1. User initiates submission of Tribute on the Network via the Wallet App.
  2. Tribute Smart Contract on the Network ensures the initial Tribute is signed by both CRA and Wallet App using electronic fingerprints (signature hashes) and verifies there're no duplicates of the CRs in Tributes among existing on the network.
  3. All daily Tributes are burned after all daily Lysis and a Touch are over.
